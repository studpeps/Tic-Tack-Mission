How To Play?

Play a simple game of Tic-Tac-Toe


Astronaut vs Space Bot

Compete with Artificial Intelligence.

Who Starts?: Select who starts playing the game.

Select your opponent: Choose difficulty level of the game.

lieutenent: very easy

major: easy

colonel: medium

marshal: hard


Astronaut vs Astronaut

Compete with your friend, with AI for rescue.

Life Gaurd: Let the computer play a turn, when you get stuck.

1, 2 and 3 Life Gaurds per player for 3x3, 4x4 and 5x5 respectively.


Select your grid size

3x3: Player who draws 3 symbols in a row, whether horizontally, vertically or diagonally wins

4x4: Player who draws 4 symbols in a row, whether horizontally, vertically or diagonally wins

5x5: Player who draws 4 symbols in a row, whether horizontally, vertically or diagonally wins
